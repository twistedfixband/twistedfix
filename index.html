TWISTED FIX

And when all is said and done, who lays you bare, my Twisted Fix?

Twisted Fix delivers a vibrant blend of British indie, pop, and punk influences, capturing the complexities and contradictions of youth in the 21st century.

With songwriting inspired by the 60s and an angst/hedonism inspired by the 90s, their music explores themes of love, loneliness, regret, and the monotony of modern life, with introspective yet catchy lyrics, melodic vocals, lush harmonies, and infectious guitar riffs.
